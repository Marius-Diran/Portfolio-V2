About Me.

Hi, I'm Emmanuel, a frontend engineer with a passion for crafting beautiful and user-friendly interfaces. My journey began in 2018 as a graphic designer, where I developed an eye for detail and a drive to create aesthetically pleasing designs. As I delved deeper into the world of design, I became fascinated with the idea of bringing my creations to life on the web and mobile devices. This curiosity led me to transition into Frontend engineering, where I could combine my design skills with my newfound love for coding. Since then, I've had the privilege of working on numerous exciting projects, each one presenting a new challenge and opportunity to grow. Currently, I'm a frontend engineer at Seerbit, a payment gateway company, where I'm responsible for building fast, secure, and user-friendly interfaces for our customers. When I'm not coding, you can find me playing video games, watching football, or indulging in historical documentaries. Throughout my journey, I've learned that good design is not just about aesthetics; it's about creating an experience that resonates with users. As a frontend engineer, I strive to bridge the gap between design and development, crafting interfaces that are both beautiful and functional.

When I am not coding, I enjoy reading, playing video games, and spending time with my family.
